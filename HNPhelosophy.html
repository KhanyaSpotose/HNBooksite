HUMAN NAVIGATION

The HN Philosophy 

At the age of 17, Hlumelo finished his matric and moved to a city called Port Elizabeth. That is where he got the freedom to further discover himself and start officialising his dream. He started doing an official business project when he was given an opportunity to co-run an online magazine for a multi-million rand national students accommodation company called South Point. He proposed to have the position, he got it and he successfully co-ran the online magazine. It went from being online to being printed. Since he knew that his dream was bigger than him, he decided to change his desired empire from Hlumelo Nyaluza to Human Navigation to allow everyone to relate to it even when he's long gone.

The empire also turned into a philosophy. The Human Navigation empire or philosophy is about empowering the extremely driven individuals that want to be industry leaders in respective fields. So, a Human Navigator is an individual that leads other peers in a field, showing them the path to the future. Hlumelo decided to carry out his main mission, of empowerment, through various avant-garde business ventures that he has designed and more commercial business projects of the future. 

After co-running an online magazine for a multi-million Rand company, he started HN Clothing which was there to allow HN's target market to stand out from the rest (making affordable clothing that were extremely different from the other brands but wearable). He used the HN Clothing project to officially start and push the Human Navigation empire/philosophy. 

From the success of the awareness, Hlumelo was featured in several media interviews covering million of people in audience and HN started to be a movement as there were people that wanted to be a part of it. From HN Clothing, he designed an initiative called the Long Tie to Success which had a 3 metre long tie that was signed by more than twenty national celebrities to further inspire HN's target market..

From the success of the Long Tie to Success he got a radio gig as the breakfast show host on SA's second biggest online radio stations. As he likes challenging himself and being against the norm, he decided to host a 27 hour show on Mandela day, 2015. He spent 27 hours on radio inspiring the nations.

The show was a success as more than 13 countries outside South Africa tuned into the show and he unofficially broke the national record for most consecutive hours spent on air by a single deejay.

From the success of the show he decided to do his first ever commercial projects that will empower the world's visionaries, raise funds for his business endeavours that will further empower the underground visionaries and also raise funds to assist HN's target market financially in their endeavours as well. He wrote the world's most uniquely structured book in the current market titled Navigate Humans: Be Against Normality.op app.
